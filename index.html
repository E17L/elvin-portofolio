Hi — I'm Elvin Sumitra, a designer who believes every brand deserves a visual language that speaks before words do.

Based in Indonesia, I work at the intersection of aesthetics and function — translating brand stories into systems that live across social platforms, screens, and first impressions. My work is defined by editorial precision, purposeful restraint, and an obsession with the details that make the difference between forgettable and iconic.

I collaborate with startups, established brands, and creative agencies to build identities with staying power — from the mark that anchors everything, to the interface someone uses every day.
